Fit Kicks

Fit Kicks was my graduation project to kick start this project I decided to focus Primarily on customisation and designing a modular shoe, my initial concept was designing a completely modular shoe sole, upper etc being completely separate and being constructed by the consumer. This also meant that the parts could be brought individually so consumers could customise and design their own footwear or repair them, making this concept highly sustainable.

Unfortunately it proved more difficult to pull off in the project time frame, I came up with various methods for construction but I was not happy with the methods I had produced. I decided that I needed to make a design decision to put that aside and move onto a different direction and focus more on my probe data moving to injury, health and modular, and design footwear based around these frameworks.
