SKETCH BOX

DESIGN BRIEF

Develop a new or improved product concept to address the following issues associated with the design of a lunch box.

The opportunity for 'buy-in' from parents and teachers

The emerging trend of a lunch box culture and a shift towards non disposable high end lunchboxes

Presenting food in a better way and food organisation/compartment options to cater for the variety of healthy food in a child's school lunch

DESIGN INTENT

To design a lunch box which is customizable to add value to the mass produced lunchbox. The construction will be designed to be robust and for the ease of cleaning including being dishwasher/freezer safe, I will also incorporate an Eco aspect into the design and materials.
