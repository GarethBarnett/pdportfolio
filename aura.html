AURA - CONCENTRIC COOKERY RINGS

DESIGN BRIEF

Culinary Enjoyment: Inspiring and motivating solutions within culinary enjoyment, which can be designed to support the desire for healthier lifestyles in future homes?

DESIGN INTENT

My response to the Electrolux brief was to design a product for apartment/single living situations where space is an issue, removing the need for a large built in stove top. The product will add enjoyment to the culinary experience by reducing the dreaded task of cleaning up mass amounts of pots and pans to create a single meal allowing for a much more relaxing experience encouraging users to cook more and enrich the process.
