NIGHT GUARDIAN

The Night guardian offers an opportunity to promote LED as a portable light guider for the elderly, but adding value to what is currently available on the market. Unlike the current night lights on the market the night guardian expresses beauty with its modern sleek appearance and quality. The Night Guardian adds a lightweight alternative to switching the main lights on during the night, instead promoting a light soft flood of warm light over the floor without the push of a button.
